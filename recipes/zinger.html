Description

A zinger burger is a popular fast food item known for its crispy and spicy chicken patty, typically served in a soft burger bun with lettuce, mayonnaise, and sometimes other toppings like tomatoes and onions. It's a signature menu item offered by various fast-food chains worldwide, notably KFC (Kentucky Fried Chicken).

The term "zinger" usually refers to the spicy flavor profile of the chicken patty, which is achieved through a combination of marination and breading with spices like paprika, cayenne pepper, and black pepper. The chicken is often deep-fried to achieve a crunchy texture on the outside while remaining juicy on the inside.

Overall, a zinger burger offers a satisfying combination of savory, spicy, and crunchy elements, making it a favorite choice for fast food enthusiasts.

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk and spices, refrigerate.
  2. Dredge marinated chicken in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs
  3. Fry breaded chicken in hot oil until golden brown and cooked through
  4. Spread mayo and hot sauce on buns, add lettuce, tomato, onion, fried chicken, and pickles
  5. Enjoy your crispy homemade zinger burgers with your favorite sides!
